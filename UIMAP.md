## Connexion - UI Map for App Designer

Purpose: This document explains every screen the prototype should include. The goal is to create a clean, modern investment marketplace app.

## 1. Splash Screen

- Connexion Logo

• Tagline: 'Where Businesses Meet Investors'

- Get Started Button

## 2. Login / Signup

Choose User Type:

- Business

- Investor

Then Login or Sign Up.

## 3. Create Profile

Business: Business Name, Founder, Industry, Description, Year, Location, Logo, Cover Image.

Investor: Name, Company, Industries, Investment Range, Preferred Stage, Location, Profile Photo.

## 4. Home

Search Bar, Categories, Trending Businesses, Star Business of the Week, PitchReels, Recommended Businesses, Bottom Navigation (Home, Discover, Pitch, Chat, Profile).

## 5. PitchReels

Instagram-style vertical videos (30–60 sec). Each reel shows Business Name, Industry, Funding Needed, Views, Interested Button, Visit Profile.

## 6. Business Profile

Business details, Funding Required, Equity Offered, Business Performance (Revenue, Profit, Growth, Customers, Employees), Graphs, Pitch Deck, Certificates, Images, Timeline, Buttons: Interested, Chat, Book Meeting.

## 7. Discover

Filters: Industry, Funding Amount, Revenue, Location, Business Stage, Growth.

## 8. Chat & Meetings

Secure chat between investor and business. Meeting scheduler with date, time and online/offline options.

## 9. Dashboards

Business Dashboard: Views, Reel Views, Interested Investors, Meetings, Messages, Funding

Progress. Investor Dashboard: Recommended Businesses, Saved Businesses, Meetings.


## 10. Notifications

Profile Viewed, Investor Interested, Meeting Request, New Message, Star Business, Verification Updates.

## 11. Verification

Upload GST, MSME, Startup Registration and Business Documents. Show Verified Badge after approval.

## Special Features

- PitchReels

- Star Business of the Week

- Shark Tank-style business metrics (Revenue, Profit, Growth, Equity, Funding)

- Secure Chat

- Meeting Scheduler

- Analytics Graphs

- Interested Button

- Verified Badges

## Overall Flow

Splash Login Choose User Type Create Profile Home Discover/PitchReels Business Profile Interested Chat Meeting
